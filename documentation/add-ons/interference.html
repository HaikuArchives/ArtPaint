The Interference add-on

This add-on produces an interference pattern from two wave centers.
You set Center A by left-clicking into the canvas, Center B by right-clicking. The setup window has their X/Y coordinates for fine-tuning.

You can change their Wavelength there as well.

Normally, the fore- and background colors are used for wave peaks and troughs, but you can also force Grayscale.
