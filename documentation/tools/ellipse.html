The ellipse tool

This tool creates an ellipse.
If you don't activate Fill ellipse, only an outline is drawn.

With the Mode you choose how to draw the ellipse:
With Corner to corner your click sets a corner, with Center to corner your click sets the center of the ellipse. Then you move the mouse to create its shape and release the mouse button when you got the ellipse you want.
Hold SHIFT to create a circle

Enable antialiasing to avoid jagged edges.
