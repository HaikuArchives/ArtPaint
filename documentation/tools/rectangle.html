The rectangle tool

This tool creates a rectangle.
If you don't activate Fill rectangle, only an outline is drawn.

With the Mode you choose how to draw the rectangle:
With Corner to corner your click sets a corner, with Center to corner your click sets the center of the rectangle. Then you move the mouse to create its shape and release the mouse button when you got the rectangle you want.
Hold SHIFT to create a square.

Enable rotation and you get to rotate the rectangle after you've drawn it.
Hold SHIFT to snap to 10Â° steps.

Enable antialiasing to avoid jagged edges.
