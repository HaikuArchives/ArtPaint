The Rectangle tool - R

This tool creates a rectangle.
You can adjust the Outline width with the slider, or choose to Fill rectangle instead.

Enable rotation and you get to rotate the rectangle after you've drawn it.
Holding SHIFT snaps to 22.5Â° angles.

Enable antialiasing to avoid jagged edges.

Holding SHIFT creates a square, holding ALT creates the rectangle from center-to-corner instead of from corner-to-corner.

The quick key to choose the aribrush tool is R.
