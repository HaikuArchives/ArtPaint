The menus

Most menus and their items are standard or don't need much explanation. Also, a short help text appears in the status bar, if you hover over an item. Let's just move quickly through them and concentrate on the not immediately obvious ones:

ArtPaint

Everything related to the application itself:
Start a New project…, open the Settings… panel, this User manual…, info About ArtPaint, and Close the current project or Quit ArtPaint altogether.

File

All the file handling:
Open/Save… image and project files, the latter are specific to ArtPaint and contain layers and meta data. For quick access, the submenus for Recent images/projects list recently opened files.

Edit

Undo/Redo the last action (the number of reversible actions can be adjusted in the settings).

You can Cut or Copy the selection of the Active layer or All layers from their submenus. If there's no selection, the whole layer gets cut or copied.

The copied selection can then be Paste as a new layer or Paste as a new project.

Delete will remove all pixels from the selection (or the whole layer if there's no selection). You can also press DEL.

As with cut and copy, you can Rotate…, Translate…, Scale… and Flip horizontally/vertically the selection of the Active layer or All layers from their submenus. If there's no selection, the whole layer gets transformed.

Rotate…, Translate… and Scale… pop up a window to enter angle, x/y coordinates or dimensions manually, or by rotating/moving/resizing it all directly on the canvas.
A right-click sets the rotation point. Holding SHIFT snaps to 22.5° angles.

Selection

You can select All of the layer, or only All non-transparent pixels. You can un-select everything with None or Invert the selection.

Selections can also Grow and Shrink by one pixel.

You can Rotate…, Translate… and Scale… the selection. Those work just like their counterparts under Edit, but are applied to the selection itself, not the selected pixels of the image.

With Hide borders the ant-line demarcating the selection can be turned off/on, in case you need an infestation-free view of your work of art.

Layer

Add creates a new layer. You can Delete, Duplicate or Merge down (i.e. merge with one below) the currently active layer.

Canvas

Rotate ±90° will rotate the whole canvas clockwise/counter-clockwise.

Resize… and Crop… open a panel to enter values manually, but you can also manipulate the canvas directly in the window. Hold SHIFT to lock the aspect ratio.

Window

Zoom in/out or Set zoom level between 25% and 800%.

Set grid lets you activate an invisible grid of 2x2, 4x4 or 8x8 pixels.

Last is a list of the special panels which are described under The other windows. It's a good idea to memorize the shortcuts to them.
